6. Methodology Reference

Classification follows a two-stage pipeline: (1) a Claude-based regulatory agent analyses the system description against EU AI Act (Regulation 2024/1689) provisions, focusing on Article 5 (prohibited practices) and Annex III (high-risk categories), with cross-references to MDR/IVDR for SaMD; (2) a static rules engine validates the agent's output against keyword heuristics and compliance JSON schemas and escalates — but never downgrades — the risk level.
